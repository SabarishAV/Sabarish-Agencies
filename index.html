About Us



Sabarish Agencies is a wholesale and retail outlet of Sonata ice cream. We offer a wide variety of ice cream flavors, including chocolate, vanilla, strawberry, mango, and many more. We are committed to providing our customers with the highest quality ice cream and the best possible customer service. We are proud to be a part of the Sonata ice cream family. Sonata is business that has been making ice cream for over several years. We use only the freshest ingredients and the highest quality standards to create our ice cream.We are committed to providing our customers with a delicious and refreshing ice cream experience. We hope you will visit us soon and see for yourself why we are the best ice cream in town!
